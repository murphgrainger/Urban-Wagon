Guide Book
General Play Your group has to complete a certain number of tasks to reach the end of the trail. You can select a specific player to complete each task, or skip it. Beware, skipping too many tasks may cause a hardship to present itself to a randomized player. You lose the game if no one is eligible to complete a task to progress the group.
Tasks Tasks are designed for players to meet new people. They can be anything from asking a "Would You Rather" question, to getting a business card. You can complete, fail, or skip a task. The only way to progress down the trail is to complete tasks. Morale increases for the player who completes the task.
Hardships If you skip or fail too many tasks, someone on your team might encounter a hardship. Player morale will decrease when faced with a hardship. Beware - some hardships may demand that a player rest. During this time they are unable to complete tasks.
Players Player morale can increase or decrease during the game. Beware - if a player's morale slips below 'Poor' they will die. Resting players and dead players cannot complete tasks for the group.
